About

Hi! I'm Óscar Maganto Torres, the web developer and Formula E fan responsible for maintaining this site. I created FE Stats to compile all the officially available data from every race weekend and make it available in easy to digest tables and graphs.

Like many viewers, I kept hearing all sorts of stats from the commentators and always wondered where I could check those for myself. Sadly, there isn't a site that compiles all FE information, and understandably so, there is just too much data. So I took on the challenge of making such a site myself.

Compiling and processing all the information of the championship since its very inception was not an easy task. As such, it is very likely that the way in which the information is provided could be improved, that some of the information is inaccurate or that new statistics could be shown. In that case, please reach me out at: email@domain.tld
